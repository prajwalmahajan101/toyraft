## What surprised us

- **golangci-lint v2 schema was a non-starter on the CI image.** The plan assumed v2; v1 + an explicit linter set was the working path. ADR-0003 now locks that in so we don't drift back.
- **commitlint default body line-length (100 chars)** rejected the PR-body Co-Authored-By footer convention. Subject rules (â‰¤72) are the load-bearing invariant; body wrap is style, not correctness. Relaxed body length, kept subject strict.
- **`.planning/` is genuinely local.** Two PRs (#2, #3) spent cycles on `.planning/` files leaking into commits before the gitignore was made airtight. Lesson: when a directory is meant to be local-only, prove the gitignore with `git check-ignore` before staging anything else.
